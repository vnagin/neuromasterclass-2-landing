Usage
  • <html data-theme="light"> — force light mode.
  • <html data-theme="dark"> — force dark (default).
  • No attribute — follows prefers-color-scheme.
  • Teal on light is #009E8A (4.5:1 on white, AA). On dark is #00E5C7 (12.5:1).
  • All semantic colors have light-mode variants with higher contrast.
